What is this place?
When Henry Molaison died, they put his brain to the medical equivalent of a industrial meat slicer. His brain was indexed in a couple thousand MRI scans.

Henry had anterograde amnesia. His brain provides most of all we now know about memory.

_

We're keen to distinguish ourselves from AI. While we tend to differentiate ourselves by how we think, maybe the real difference is in what we consume.

For now, here's how our inputs differ:
  • our inputs are multimodal: visuals, audio, touch, taste, scent
  • our inputs are others: friends, family, acquaintances
  • our inputs are serendipitous
_

For the past three years, I've put down a journal entry every day about what I saw, heard, did, experienced, thought about. Here's that data. Open source.
